How to play the 2048 game

To play the game, use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the tiles in the desired direction. As you slide the tiles, look for opportunities to merge tiles that have the same number. When two identical tiles collide, they will combine to form a larger tile, effectively doubling their value. Your ultimate goal is to strategically maneuver the tiles to reach the coveted 2048 tile. Along the way, aim to achieve the highest score possible by continuously merging tiles and creating larger numbers. Remember, careful planning and foresight are key to mastering the game and achieving victory!
